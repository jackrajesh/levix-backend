About LEVIX

Founded in India with a vision to revolutionize the local economy, LEVIX is the first AI-native commerce platform built specifically for Indian Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). We understand that for millions of business owners, WhatsApp is not just a messaging app—it's their storefront.

Our mission is to democratize high-end AI technology, making it accessible to every restaurant, retail shop, salon, and clinic across the country. LEVIX automates the complex dance of WhatsApp commerce, from taking initial orders to providing 24/7 customer support.

By leveraging advanced natural language processing, LEVIX acts as an AI ordering assistant that never sleeps, never forgets a customer, and never misses a sale. We are committed to helping local businesses scale their operations, reduce manual workload, and drive significant growth in an increasingly digital world.

Whether you're a neighborhood grocery store or a bustling city cafe, LEVIX is here to ensure your business stays ahead of the curve. Join the thousands of businesses using LEVIX to build the future of Indian retail.
